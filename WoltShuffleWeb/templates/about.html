About Wolt Shuffle

Wolt Shuffle is a Django based web-app, written in Python, meant to generate random Wolt dishes, from restauraunts near you.
In the Shuffle page you are presented with dish's image, name, price, description, and the restaurant's name in bold.

Wolt Shuffle scrapes the Wolt website (with the address of the current user) to return the shuffled dish.
Since wolt has no API, Fiddler was used to analyze Wolt's traffic.

Because Scraping is a time consuming process, the first few shuffles will be a bit slower. In those shuffles, Wolt Shuffle will cache your data, and before you know it, you'll shuffle like the wind. Wolt Shuffle logs for each user the dishes he pressed "NEVER AGAIN" on, and never offers them again to the user.
